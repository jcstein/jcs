About

I work on the Developer Experience team at Celestia Labs, focusing on documentation, demos, and hackathons. I'm also an editor for the Celestia Improvement Proposal process, which you can get involved in at forum.celestia.org.

My journey into tech started during the pandemic while finishing my BS in Business (Supply Chain Management & Analytics) at VCU. I discovered web development, then blockchain, and never looked back.

Previously: Ankr (DevRel Engineer), buildspace (TA), co-founder of Lattice Supply. Active in Developer DAO and Peeple DAO.
